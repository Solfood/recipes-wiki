# Award-Worthy Tri-Tip Chili

## Overview
Deep, rich beef chili built around cubed tri-tip, masa harina, and Mexican chocolate. Savory, mildly bitter from the chiles, gently warm with spice, and balanced with just enough acidity to pop.

Improves overnight and freezes extremely well.

## Yield
Serves 6–8

## Ingredients

### Meat
- 2½ lb tri-tip, trimmed and cut into 1–1½ inch cubes

### Aromatics
- 2 large yellow onions, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 roasted poblano pepper, diced

### Chiles & Spices
- 3 tbsp ancho-forward chili powder
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp ground cinnamon
- ½–1 tsp cayenne, to taste
- 1½ tsp kosher salt (plus more to finish)
- 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper

### Liquids
- ~4 cups beef stock

### Thickeners & Finishers
- 2 tbsp masa harina
- 1 oz Mexican chocolate (Ibarra-style), finely chopped
- ¾ tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1–2 tsp brown sugar (optional)

### Optional Late Add-Ins
- 1 tbsp freeze-dried shallots (or 1 small fresh shallot, minced)
